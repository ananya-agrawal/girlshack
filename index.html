Who We Are

Hacks

Hacks is a non profitable, student held hackathon to introduce young girls across India to coding through the experience of hackathon. With women vastly underrepresented in tech industry all over the world, Hacks strives to inspire a new wave of Indian female coders to bring equality to the booming industry.

On the occasion of Women's Day, we call all female programmers to witness 36 hours of coding, developing, learning and fun. The hackathon is open to all females who believe that they can make something that will make a difference to society as a whole. Hacks will not just be a 36 hours hackathon but a collaborative learning experience where you will surely walk away with new knowledge to rule the world.
